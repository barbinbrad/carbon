# Carbon ERP

<Features />

Carbon ERP is a high permformance, [open-source](https://github.com/barbinbrad/carbon), single-tenant (incomplete) ERP. It allows customers, suppliers, and employees to share a common platform.

## Why Carbon ERP?

Currently you shouldn't use Carbon ERP. It is incomplete and not ready for production. However, it is a great learning tool for those who want to learn about business software. It is also a great tool for those who want to work with the latest technologies.
